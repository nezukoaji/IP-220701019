Robotics Workshop

Date: February 15, 2024 | Location: REC Robotics Lab

In this interactive workshop, participants will learn the fundamentals of robotics, including basic electronics, mechanics, and programming. This hands-on experience will allow you to build and control your own robot by the end of the session.

  • Introduction to robotics and automation
  • Hands-on robot building session
